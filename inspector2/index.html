PDF Inspector

Soon on the Mac App Store!

PDF Inspector is a powerful tool to manage your links within a PDF. It quickly and clearly displays both internal and external links and lets you edit them with the click of a button. Alternatively, you can get an overview your links by exporting them to a CSV file — making it easy to find duplicates, overlapping hotspots, missing destinations, and more.

What It Does

PDF Inspector works with PDF metadata and link annotations, providing automated tools to track down and address common issues with PDF files.
